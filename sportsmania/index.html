ABOUT US

The Vidyalankar Institute of Technology Sports Council is a dynamic body committed to fostering a culture of sports and physical fitness within the institute. Dedicated to promoting teamwork, discipline, and a healthy lifestyle, the council organizes various sporting events and activities throughout the academic year. It aims to provide students with opportunities to excel in both individual and team sports, nurturing their overall development. The Sports Council strives to create a vibrant sports community, encouraging participation from all students and promoting a sense of sportsmanship and camaraderie.
