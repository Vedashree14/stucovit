About Us

VERVE VIT is an exhilarating intercollegiate festival hosted by VIT, Mumbai, bringing together students from diverse academic backgrounds to showcase their talents & celebrate creativity. With a vibrant mix of cultural events VERVE VIT offers a platform for participants to engage in friendly competition, fostering a spirit of collaboration. The festival encompasses a wide array of activities like music performances, dance competitions, street plays and much more. Known for its energetic atmosphere and innovative events, VERVE VIT has become a highlight on the academic calendar, attracting participants and spectators alike, creating lasting memories within VIT.
